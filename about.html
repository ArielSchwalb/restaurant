How the Journey Started...

In January of 2022, a few friends got together for dinner and started making some plans. While there were a ton of vegan cafes in Tel Aviv, few, if any, were focused on international cuisine, and almost no restaurant had any connection to restaurants in the Arab community outside of Israel. That's when Ariel's vegan cafe was born. Ariel's cafe is committed to serving both kosher and Halal food. We operate throughout the Middle East. We hope to bring everyone together over everyone's favorite thing... food!
